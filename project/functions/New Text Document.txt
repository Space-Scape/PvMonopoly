ROLL CODES
innit - EU dice roll
ainit - US dice roll

ADMIN CONSOLE --- F2
roll eu - adds a roll available for EU player
roll us - adds a roll available for US player
buy us - places a house on the current US tile
buy eu - places a house on the current EU tile
sell us - removes a house from the current US tile
sell eu - removes a house from the current EU tile
housetest - test houses (input multiple times for more houses)
teletest - test teleports
